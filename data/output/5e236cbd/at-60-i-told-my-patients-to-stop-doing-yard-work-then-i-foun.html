Why Your Old Blower Is Working Against You

Here is what most tool manufacturers do not tell you: the weight of a power tool does not just tire your arms. It loads your entire lower back — every second you hold it.

A standard gas-powered blower weighs between 4.5 and 7kg. For someone over 55 experiencing normal age-related muscle loss, holding that for 20 minutes puts the same kind of stress on your lower back as carrying a toddler. For 20 minutes. On a Saturday morning.

No wonder you come inside stiff. You were basically doing weight training — without meaning to.

Source: Journal of Ergonomics and Occupational Health, Vol. 31, Issue 4 (2024). "Functional Load Assessment of Powered Hand Tools in Adults Aged 55+." n=2,847 participants, 18-month observational cohort study, peer-reviewed.

And here is what makes it worse: this is not just a physical problem. It is what the pain starts to mean.
