Why Your Old Tools Are Quietly Stealing Your Independence

Here's what nobody says out loud: the real cost of a gas blower isn't the price tag. It's what it does to your body.

A standard gas blower weighs between 15 and 25 pounds. Before you even use it, you yank a starter cord — a sudden, jerking motion that physical therapists classify as high-risk for anyone over 55. Then you carry it for 20 to 30 minutes, one shoulder bearing the vibration, your lower back absorbing every step.

And let's not talk about the gasoline, the oil, the annual tune-up, or the three failed cold-morning starts when you're already freezing in your driveway.
